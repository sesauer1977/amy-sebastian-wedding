# Amy & Sebastian Wedding Website — Design Brainstorm

## Three Stylistic Approaches

### Approach A — "Coastal Elegance"
A refined, airy aesthetic drawing from the East Lothian coastline: soft sea-glass greens, warm sand tones, and crisp whites. Feels like a long weekend at a Scottish country estate.
**Probability:** 0.07

### Approach B — "Scottish Gothic Romance"
Dark, moody, and atmospheric. Deep charcoal and forest greens with gold accents, inspired by Dirleton Castle and the dramatic Scottish coast. Dramatic serif typography.
**Probability:** 0.03

### Approach C — "Timeless Ivory & Sage"
A warm, editorial-style layout with ivory backgrounds, sage green accents, and dusty rose highlights. Feels like a beautifully printed invitation come to life — classic, romantic, unhurried.
**Probability:** 0.09

---

## Chosen Approach: C — "Timeless Ivory & Sage"

### Design Movement
Editorial romanticism — the aesthetic of a beautifully curated wedding magazine spread, where every element feels considered and unhurried. Inspired by fine stationery and the warmth of Scottish country houses.

### Core Principles
1. **Warmth over coldness** — ivory and sage replace stark white and grey; every surface feels inviting
2. **Typographic storytelling** — large, expressive serif headlines carry the emotional weight; body text is calm and readable
3. **Generous whitespace** — sections breathe; nothing is crowded or rushed
4. **Subtle coastal references** — soft textures and muted tones nod to the East Lothian coast without being literal

### Color Philosophy
- **Ivory** `oklch(0.97 0.012 85)` — primary background; warm, paper-like, not clinical white
- **Deep Forest** `oklch(0.28 0.06 155)` — primary text and headings; rich, grounded
- **Sage Green** `oklch(0.62 0.07 155)` — accent color; the ownable brand hue, evoking the Scottish landscape
- **Dusty Rose** `oklch(0.78 0.06 15)` — secondary accent; warmth and romance
- **Warm Charcoal** `oklch(0.35 0.01 80)` — secondary text; readable without harshness

### Layout Paradigm
Asymmetric editorial layout with generous vertical rhythm. Sections alternate between full-bleed imagery and text-heavy panels. Navigation is minimal and sticky. No symmetrical grid boxes — instead, flowing, magazine-style composition.

### Signature Elements
1. **Thin horizontal rules** with a small floral motif dividing sections
2. **Large italic serif pull-quotes** for key information (date, venue)
3. **Soft-edged image frames** with slight shadow, never hard-cropped

### Interaction Philosophy
Interactions are calm and deliberate. Hover states are gentle (subtle underlines, slight opacity shifts). Scroll-triggered fade-ins are slow and graceful. Nothing feels rushed or flashy.

### Animation
- Entrance animations: `opacity: 0 → 1` with `translateY(20px → 0)`, 600ms ease-out, staggered 80ms per element
- Navigation: smooth scroll with 400ms ease
- Password gate: elegant fade-in/out
- No bouncing, no aggressive motion

### Typography System
- **Display / Headlines:** `Cormorant Garamond` — elegant, high-contrast serif with romantic character
- **Body / UI:** `Lato` — clean, readable, neutral; pairs beautifully with Cormorant
- **Accent / Dates:** `Cormorant Garamond Italic` — for dates, pull-quotes, and special callouts
- Hierarchy: Display 72px → H1 48px → H2 32px → H3 24px → Body 16px

### Brand Essence
A private, intimate digital invitation for the people who matter most — where every detail of a Scottish coastal wedding is presented with warmth and clarity.
**Personality:** Warm · Unhurried · Elegant

### Brand Voice
Headlines and CTAs feel like a personal letter, not a corporate announcement.
- *"Join us on the East Lothian coast as we begin our life together."*
- *"Everything you need to know — and nothing you don't."*
No generic filler. No "Welcome to our website."

### Wordmark & Logo
A delicate monogram intertwining "A" and "C" (for Amy Carmichael) with a small sprig of heather — hand-drawn style, never block initials. Note: initials "SS" are explicitly avoided.

### Signature Brand Color
**Sage Green** `oklch(0.62 0.07 155)` — unmistakably this wedding's colour.
